## What The Audit Includes

### 1. Location-by-Location Money Leak Map

A plain-English map of where revenue is leaking by location, provider, service line, and patient segment where the data allows.

Focus areas:

- No-shows and same-day cancellations.
- Completed visits without a next appointment.
- Missed or mishandled inbound.
- Dormant patients who should be reactivated.

### 2. Patient Revenue Recovery Analysis

A quantified analysis of annualized recoverable revenue opportunity from existing services and pricing.

The analysis will define:

- Baseline show-up rate.
- Baseline rebooking rate.
- Dormant patient pool.
- Estimated recovery opportunity.
- Data quality gaps that need to be fixed.

### 3. 90-Day Retention Sprint Roadmap

A prioritized implementation plan for the first 90 days.

Each recommendation will include:

- The leak it addresses.
- The estimated revenue impact.
- The system or behavior required.
- The owner and staff lift.
- The recommended sequence.

### 4. Owner Summary

A one-page summary for the owner, partner, lender, buyer, or leadership team.

It will answer:

- Where is the money leaking?
- How much is realistically recoverable?
- What should be fixed first?
- What should not be touched yet?

---

## What The Audit Does Not Include

The Audit does not include:

- Paid ads, SEO, funnels, or lead generation.
- Social media management.
- New practice software rollout.
- EMR migration.
- Membership or subscription design.
- Hiring, compensation, injector retention, or HR.
- Inventory, finance, or broad operations consulting.

The Audit is focused on **money from patients you already have, via visits that should have happened and did not.**

---

## Data Needed

To complete the Audit, Allenix needs:

- 12 months of scheduling data for all in-scope locations.
- 12 months of revenue data for all in-scope locations.
- No-show and cancellation data where available.
- Patient visit history or de-identified cohort data.
- Current patient communication templates where available.
- Current systems list: scheduling, messaging, call handling, CRM / patient records.

De-identified data is acceptable where appropriate.

## Guarantee

If the Audit does not identify at least **5x the Audit fee** in annualized, reasonably recoverable revenue opportunity based on historical data, you may elect not to pay the Audit fee.

For a $4,800 Audit, that means Allenix must identify at least **$24,000** in annualized, reasonably recoverable revenue opportunity.

---

## Guarantee Conditions

For the guarantee to apply:

- You provide at least 12 months of historical scheduling and revenue data for all in-scope locations within 7 days of Audit start.
- You or a designated decision-maker attend the Audit findings meeting within 21 days of Audit start.
- Recoverable revenue is limited to improvements in show-up rate, rebooking rate, response to inbound inquiries, and reactivation of dormant patients on existing services and pricing.
- Recoverable revenue does not include hypothetical new locations, new services, or price increases.
- If you believe the 5x threshold was not met, you notify Allenix during the findings meeting or in writing within 7 days of that meeting.
