# Medical Spa & Aesthetic Practice Operational Workflows Research

## Executive Summary

This document outlines the specific operational workflows in medical spas and aesthetic practices where AI/automation could deliver massive ROI for Grand Slam offers. Research indicates that medical spas lose 20-30% of potential revenue due to operational inefficiencies, primarily in patient communication, scheduling, and follow-up workflows.

## Key Industry Statistics (2025-2026)

- **30% of calls go unanswered** in medical spas (Zenoti data)
- **50% of online bookings are abandoned** before completion
- **No-show rates average 15-25%** industry-wide
- **Providers spend 2 hours/day** on documentation and notes
- **Patient inquiries left for 10 minutes go cold**
- **Average revenue growth of 45%** within 6 months when implementing proper automation (Mindbody data)
- **26% increase in bookings** within 6 months of implementing comprehensive practice management (Mindbody data)
