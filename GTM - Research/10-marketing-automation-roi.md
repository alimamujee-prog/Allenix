# Prompt 10: Medical Spa Marketing Automation - Lowest Effort, Highest ROI

Research marketing automation and lead generation tactics for medical spas, specifically **ranking them by implementation effort vs revenue impact**.

## Marketing Automation Types to Analyze

1. **Google Ads automation and smart bidding**
2. **Facebook/Instagram automated ad campaigns**
3. **Email marketing sequences and drip campaigns**
4. **SMS marketing automation**
5. **Review generation automation**
6. **Referral program automation**
7. **Social media scheduling and AI content tools**
8. **Chatbots and website lead capture**
9. **Retargeting and remarketing**
10. **Lead nurturing sequences**

## For Each Tactic, Provide

### Implementation Metrics
- **Setup Effort:** 1-10 scale (1 = easiest, can DIY in hours)
- **Technical Difficulty:** Integration complexity
- **Monthly Cost:** Software and ad spend
- **Time to Setup:** Hours/days required

### Performance Metrics
- **Lead/Conversion Impact:** Typical improvement
- **Time to Results:** Days/weeks to see ROI
- **Ongoing Effort:** Maintenance time per week
- **Success Rate:** What % of practices see positive ROI

### Risk Assessment
- **Risk Level:** What could go wrong
- **Common Pitfalls:** Where implementations fail
- **Skill Required:** Can staff manage it or need an agency

## Categorization Framework

### Quick Wins vs Long-Term Plays
- **Quick Wins:** Tactics that work in under 30 days
- **Medium-Term:** Tactics that require 30-90 days
- **Long-Term:** Tactics that require 90+ days to mature
- **Compounders:** Tactics that improve over time

### Complexity Levels
- **DIY-Friendly:** Practice can implement themselves
- **DIY-with-Guidance:** Need some expert setup, then self-manage
- **Managed:** Requires ongoing agency or expert management

## What's Already Working

### Case Studies to Find
- Medical spas using automation successfully
- Before/after metrics (leads, bookings, revenue)
- Which tools vendors are winning
- Implementation stories (how they did it)

### Benchmark Data
- Industry averages for each tactic
- Top performer results
- What separates winners from losers

## Create a Ranked Matrix

### Visual Output
Create a matrix with:
- **X-axis:** Effort (Low → High)
- **Y-axis:** Impact (Low → High)
- **Y-axis:** Time to Results (Fast → Slow)

Plot each tactic and highlight:
- **Quadrant 1:** Low Effort, High Impact, Fast Results (DO THESE FIRST)
- **Quadrant 2:** High Effort, High Impact (DO THESE SECOND)
- **Quadrant 3:** Low Effort, Low Impact (CONSIDER IF TIME)
- **Quadrant 4:** High Effort, Low Impact (AVOID)

### Ranking Formula
Calculate **"Automation Score"** for each tactic:
- Score = (Lead Impact × Conversion Rate) ÷ (Effort × Cost × Time to Results)
- Rank from highest to lowest

## Output Format

1. **Ranked table** of all 10 tactics by Automation Score

   - Implementation steps

## Constraints

Focus on tactics that:
- Can be set up once and run with minimal ongoing effort
- Have clear ROI measurement
- Work for $1M-$10M practices
- Don't require building an in-house marketing team
- Can be managed by existing staff or a light agency retainer

## Priority

Prioritize tactics that:
- Generate immediate leads or bookings
- Have clear attribution
- Scale with spend (more spend = more results)
- Don't require major content creation
- Integrate with existing practice management tools
