# Prompt 6: Medical Spa Quick Wins - Low Effort, High Impact Opportunities

Research the medical spa and aesthetic practice market to identify specific operational and marketing improvements that are:
- **Easy to implement** (low technical complexity, minimal disruption, fast rollout)
- **High ROI** (significant revenue impact or cost savings within 30-90 days)
- **Low risk** (minimal downside, reversible if needed)

I need you to find and **RANK** the top 10-15 "quick wins" for medical spas.

## Areas to Investigate

### 1. No-Show & Cancellation Recovery
- What specific tactics reduce no-shows with minimal effort
- How much revenue practices recover vs effort required
- Specific tools or approaches that work best

### 2. Immediate Revenue Capture
- Low-hanging fruit for increasing bookings immediately
- Simple pricing or packaging changes that increase average order value
- Re-engaging dormant patients with minimal effort

### 3. Review & Reputation Quick Wins
- Easiest ways to generate more 5-star reviews quickly
- Simple reputation management tactics
- Impact of reviews on new patient acquisition

### 4. Booking & Scheduling Hacks
- Simple changes that increase booking conversion
- Reducing friction in the scheduling process
- Filling cancelled slots with minimal effort

### 5. Communication Automation
- Easiest automations to implement (SMS, email, chat)
- Templates and scripts that work best
- Time savings for front desk staff

## Ranking Framework

For each opportunity, provide:
- **Effort Score** (1-10, where 1 = easiest)
- **Impact Score** (1-10, where 10 = highest ROI)
- **Time to Results** (days/weeks)
- **Implementation Cost** (software cost, setup time)
- **Risk Level** (what could go wrong)

Create a **ranked list** sorted by Impact/Effort ratio (highest first).

## Constraints

Focus on tactics that can be:
- Implemented in under 2 weeks
- Less than $500/month in software costs
- Minimal disruption to operations
- Reversible if they don't work

## Output Format

Return a ranked table with:
| Rank | Opportunity | Effort (1-10) | Impact (1-10) | Time to ROI | Cost | Risk |
|------|------------|---------------|---------------|-------------|------|------|

Plus detailed explanations for the top 5 opportunities including:
- Specific implementation steps
- Expected results
- Real examples if available
- Potential pitfalls

## Research Focus

Look for:
- Industry best practices
- Case studies of quick implementations
- Vendor solutions for rapid deployment
- Practice owner testimonials
- Benchmark data on results

Prioritize opportunities that have been proven to work in similar practices.
