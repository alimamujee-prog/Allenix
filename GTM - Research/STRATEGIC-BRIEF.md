## What We're Doing

Launching Allenix Labs into a new vertical: **medical spas, aesthetic practices, and elective medical clinics** ($2M–$8M revenue). Adapting the existing Map-Build-Run system for this market. No clients in the vertical yet. Goal: 3 new clients/month, $5K–$15K MRR each.

---

## Strategic Decisions Made

### 1. Focus: Retention First, Not Acquisition
Everyone sells "more leads." Allenix sells "stop losing the patients you already paid to acquire." The math is the hook: a $2M practice at 50% retention leaves ~$400K/year on the table. Retention is the most differentiated play and the highest-leverage revenue lever for these owners.

### 2. Offer Structure: Entry + Core + Full
- **Retention Quick Start** ($2,500 setup + $497/mo): Automated follow-up, rebooking, no-show prevention, review requests. Entry point. 2-week deploy.
- **Practice Growth System** ($3,500 setup + $2,500–$4,000/mo): Quick Start plus win-back campaigns, AI phone receptionist, referral engine, treatment adherence, dashboard.
- **The Allenix Transformation** ($7,500–$15,000/mo): Full Map-Build-Run. Acquisition optimization, operational efficiency, real-time dashboard, dedicated success manager, Studios content.

### 3. ICP: "Priya"
42-year-old NP/physician who owns a $2M–$8M med spa. Top injector AND CEO. No CMO, no COO. Referral-driven buyer. Burned by agencies. Thinks in monthly revenue, not LTV. Speaks in specifics: "12 no-shows last week," "patients book once and never come back."

### 4. GTM Targets
- 3 new clients per month
- $5K–$15K MRR per client
- 1,200 ICP contacts reached per month
- 12 qualified conversations per month
- 25% close rate (3 of 12)

## The Execution Layer

### Funnel Math (Working Backwards from 3 Clients/Month)

```
Target: 3 closed clients
  ← 25% close rate
  ← 12 qualified conversations
  ← 92 total replies across channels
  ← 1,200 contacts reached
```

| Channel | Monthly Volume | Reply Rate | Meetings Booked |
|---------|---------------|------------|-----------------|
| Cold Email (Apollo + scraped lists) | 700 sends | 5% (35 replies) | 7 |
| LinkedIn (Sales Navigator) | 200 connections | 6% (12 replies) | 3 |
| Instagram DMs | 150 DMs | 10% (15 replies) | 2 |
| Warm / Referral / Events | 150 touches | 20% (30 replies) | 2 |
| **Total** | **1,200** | **~8% avg** | **14** |

Plan for 14 to account for no-shows and cancellations. Target 12.

### List Building Strategy

**Data sources:**
1. **Apollo.io** — 200–400 contacts/month. Filter by industry, revenue, employee count, geography, title. Provides email, phone, LinkedIn URL.
2. **LinkedIn Sales Navigator** (already in tech stack) — 300–500 contacts/month. Industry + title + location filters.
3. **Google Maps + Apify scraping** — 200–300 contacts/month. Scrape "medical spa" in target cities, enrich with owner data.
4. **AmSpa directory** — 50–100 contacts/month. Higher quality, invested owners.
5. **State licensing boards** — 50–100 contacts/month. Public records.
6. **Instagram DMs** — 100–150 contacts/month. Search hashtags, identify owners from profiles.

**ICP filter:** Revenue $1M–$10M, 5–50 employees, 1–3 locations, owner-operated (not PE, not chain), Gulf South first then expanding.

**Weekly cadence:** Monday source/enrich, Tue–Thu outreach (30 emails/day, 10 LinkedIn connections/day, 5 Instagram DMs/day), Friday follow-up and qualify.

**First three cities:** Houston (home market), Dallas-Fort Worth, San Antonio. Expand to Nashville, Birmingham, then Atlanta, Phoenix, Denver.

Full details in `LIST-BUILDING-STRATEGY.md`.

### Grand Slam Offer

The Quick Start is designed using the Hormozi Value Equation:

**Value = (Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood) / (Time Delay × Effort)**

- **Dream Outcome:** Recover $5K–$10K/month from patients who disappear
- **Perceived Likelihood:** Specific proof from named practices, clear mechanism
- **Time Delay:** First results in 2 weeks, pays for itself in 60 days
- **Effort:** One 45-minute call. Zero behavior change. No new software.

**Value stack:** $5,300/month in recovered revenue and consulting value for $497/month = 10.7x return.

**Bonuses ($1,150 value):** Free Practice Assessment, Missed Revenue Calculator, Competitor Benchmark Report, Treatment Rebooking Calendar.

**Four-layer guarantee:** (1) 3x monthly fee in 60 days or fee waived, (2) cancel anytime, (3) 10x value promise, (4) first month risk-free with setup refund.

**Next step:** Run through the Hormozi AI for stress-testing. Questions prepared in `HORMOZI-AI-QUESTIONS.md`.

Full details in `GRAND-SLAM-OFFER.md`.
