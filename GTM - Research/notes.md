# Allenix Med Spa GTM Plan — Full Review

## Date
2026-04-24

---

## The Business

Allenix installs a **Patient Revenue Recovery System** for med spa and aesthetic practice groups. Not leads. Not ads. Not software. Revenue recovery from patients they already paid to acquire.

---

## The Buyer

**Priya.** A 45-year-old NP or physician who owns a $5M-$25M multi-location med spa group. 2-5 locations. 20-100 employees. She started as the top injector, became the CEO by necessity. No CMO, no COO. Office managers run each location but nobody owns the systems across locations.

Her line: "We're busy but my bank account doesn't show it."

She's been burned by agencies. She trusts peers, not pitches. She thinks in monthly revenue, not LTV. She will not fill out a 12-field form. She will take a 30-minute call if it feels relevant.

Publicly we target **$5M-$25M multi-location groups**. Internally we accept $3M+ single-location if the pain is clearly retention.

---

## The Public Claim

**Most $5M-$25M med spa groups we model are leaking at least $150K-$600K/year in avoidable missed visits and one-and-done patients.**

We do not say "$15K-$50K/month recovered" or "80% of your patients never return" yet. Those are future claims once we have case studies.

---

## V1 Scope — Four Levers Only

The first 90 days own four problems:

1. **Show-up rate** — reduce no-shows and same-day cancels
2. **Rebooking rate** — increase completed visits that leave with a next appointment
3. **Missed/mishandled inbound** — capture unanswered calls, slow responses, dropped web/DM inquiries
4. **Dormant patient reactivation** — reactivate patients inactive 6-18 months

Everything else is later. No ads. No funnels. No SEO. No EMR migrations. No hiring. No inventory consulting. The promise: more money from patients you already have, via visits that should have happened and did not.

## Tier 1: Patient Revenue Recovery Audit ($4,800)

**Promise:** In 14 days, we show exactly where $150K-$600K/year is leaking, then hand you a 90-day plan to recover it.

**Deliverables:**

- Location-by-location Money Leak Map
- Show-up, rebooking, inbound, and reactivation analysis
- 90-day Retention Sprint roadmap with dollar values per play
- 1-page owner summary
- Audit readout call

**Three reports you keep regardless:**

1. Competitive Benchmark Report (5 closest competing practices)
2. Treatment Rebooking Calendar (12-month, customized to treatment mix)
3. Revenue Leak Calculator (customized spreadsheet for tracking)

**Guarantee:** If we don't find at least $50,000 in reasonably recoverable annual revenue based on your historical data, you don't pay. You keep the reports either way.

## Tier 2: Retention Engine Build-Out ($8K/month for 3 months)

**Promise:** In 90 days, we install the first Patient Revenue Recovery System across pilot locations.

**Value stack:**

| Component | Annualized Impact |
|---|---:|
| Treatment-specific rebooking automation | $60K-$200K/year |
| No-show and cancellation prevention | $36K-$100K/year |
| Missed inbound capture and response | $18K-$60K/year |
| Dormant patient reactivation | $50K-$200K/year |
| Weekly performance reporting | Included |
| Done-for-you implementation and management | Included |
| **Total estimated annual impact** | **$164K-$560K/year** |

Against $96K/year price. Target: 3-5x return, ideally 10x. First 90 days installs the core systems. Full year compounds value with larger initiatives built on the baseline.

After the 3-month build, two paths: step down to a **Retention Retainer** (TBD/month, monitoring and optimization) or step up to **Tier 3** (broader operations).

**Guarantee:** If the system doesn't generate at least $50,000 in annualized recovered revenue within 90 days, Allenix works for free until it does.

The $50K threshold scales with practice size. At $300K/month revenue, even a 2% improvement across the four levers clears $50K. The math works at both the $536 industry-average ticket (93 recovered appointments/year) and a conservative $300 ticket (167 recovered appointments/year). About 8-14 recovered appointments per month.

## Internal Qualification (Not Client-Facing)

Before offering the Build-Out, confirm:

- Practice does at least $300K/month in total revenue
- At least 2 active locations (single-location is Audit-only unless pain is exceptional)
- Owner or designated decision-maker is directly involved, not delegated to an office manager
- Enough appointment volume to produce a meaningful baseline

Guarantee math: $50K annualized at $300-$536 average ticket = ~93-167 recovered appointments/year (~8-14/month). At $300K/month revenue, even a 2% improvement across levers clears $50K. The revenue floor protects you; no separate appointment count rule needed.

## The Sales Motion

**One CTA everywhere:** Book a 30-minute Patient Revenue Checkup.

The Checkup is not a free consulting call. It's a live money-leak conversation that makes the paid Audit feel like the safest next step.

**Call flow (30 minutes):**

- 0-3 min: Confirm the frame — "I want to see if there's enough revenue leaking to justify a proper audit"
- 3-8 min: Surface the pain — "What happens when a patient finishes a treatment and doesn't rebook?"
- 8-15 min: Do rough retention math live — revenue, locations, average ticket, return rate
- 15-20 min: Name 3-5 likely leak points
- 20-26 min: Pitch the Audit — deliverables, included reports, guarantee
- 26-30 min: Close — "Do you want to start next week?"

**Primary close:**

"The next step is the Patient Revenue Recovery Audit. It's $4,800. If we don't find at least $50,000 in recoverable annual revenue, you don't pay."

Objection scripts are built out for: "tried agencies before," "we already have software," "my front desk handles it," "too busy," "impersonal messages," "HIPAA," "need to think about it," and "send me information."

## List Building

**Data sources:**

1. **Apollo.io** — 200-400 contacts/month, filtered by industry, revenue $5M+, 20+ employees, owner titles
2. **LinkedIn Sales Navigator** — 300-500 contacts/month (already in tech stack)
3. **Google Maps + Apify scraping** — 200-300 contacts/month, scrape "medical spa" in target cities
4. **AmSpa directory** — 50-100 contacts/month, higher quality
5. **State licensing boards** — 50-100 contacts/month, public records
6. **Instagram DMs** — 100-150 contacts/month, hashtag research

**Weekly cadence:** Monday source/enrich, Tuesday-Thursday outreach (30 emails/day, 10 LinkedIn connections/day, 5 Instagram DMs/day), Friday follow-up and qualify.

**First three cities:** Houston (home market, ~40 practices in range), Dallas-Fort Worth (~50), San Antonio (~15-20). Then Nashville, Birmingham, then Atlanta, Phoenix, Denver.
