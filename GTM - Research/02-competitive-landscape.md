# Prompt 2: Medical Spa Competitive Landscape & Technology Threats

Research the competitive landscape for medical spas and aesthetic practices in the 2025-2026 market. I need to understand:

## 1. Competitive Dynamics
- How saturated is the market in major metros vs secondary cities
- What are the primary ways practices compete (price, convenience, results, brand)
- What new types of competitors are emerging (corporate med spas, tech-enabled practices)
- Consolidation trends and roll-up strategies

## 2. Technology Adoption Patterns
- What practice management software dominates (PatientNow, Zenoti, Mindbody, etc.)
- How quickly med spas adopt new technology vs other healthcare
- What AI/automation tools are already being used successfully
- What technology have they tried and abandoned (and why)

## 3. What They Fear Most About Competition
- Losing patients to newer, tech-enabled competitors
- Being left behind in AI adoption
- Price pressure from corporate chains
- Not being able to attract/retain top talent
- Negative reviews and reputation damage

## 4. Patient Behavior Changes
- How patient expectations have changed (booking, communication, convenience)
- What patients expect now that they didn't 2-3 years ago
- How patients choose between providers
- What causes patients to switch providers

## 5. Future Threats
- Emerging technologies that could disrupt their model
- Regulatory changes on the horizon
- Economic threats (recession sensitivity, etc.)

## Research Focus
Focus on 2024-2026 trends and forward-looking analysis. Include specific examples of practices that have won or lost due to technology/competitive factors.

## Sources to Check
- Industry reports on med spa market size and growth
- Technology vendor market share reports
- Merger & acquisition activity in the space
- Patient behavior surveys
- Competitive intelligence from industry publications

## Output Format
Return findings with specific examples, case studies, and forward-looking analysis. Include both threats and opportunities.
