# Prompt 8: Medical Spa Staff Training & Documentation - Low Effort Solutions

Research the staff training, onboarding, and documentation challenges in medical spas, specifically looking for **LOW-EFFORT solutions that deliver HIGH-IMPACT results**.

## 1. Training Pain Points

### Current State
- How long it currently takes to onboard new aestheticians, injectors, front desk staff
- What training tasks consume the most owner time
- Where training falls short and causes problems
- Cost of training mistakes

### Impact
- Revenue lost during training periods
- Compliance risks from inadequate training
- Turnover impact on practice performance

## 2. Low-Effort Training Solutions

### Solutions to Investigate
- **Video training libraries and LMS platforms** (how fast to set up)
- **AI-powered training assistants**
- **Standardized procedure documentation**
- **Checklists and SOPs** that can be created once and reused
- **Peer training systems**
- **Shadowing protocols**

### For Each Solution, Analyze
- **Setup Effort:** Hours/days to implement
- **Ongoing Effort:** How much work to maintain
- **Time Savings for Staff:** Hours saved per week
- **Quality Improvement:** Reduction in errors or compliance issues
- **Cost:** Monthly or annual software costs
- **ROI Timeline:** How long to see results

## 3. Documentation Burden

### Current Challenges
- How much time providers spend on patient notes and documentation
- What documentation is required vs what's useful
- Where documentation creates bottlenecks

### Solutions to Investigate
- **AI scribe tools** and their effectiveness
- **Template-based documentation systems**
- **Voice-to-text solutions**
- **Automated note generation**

### For Each Solution, Analyze
- **Implementation Effort:** 1-10 scale
- **Time Savings:** Hours saved per provider per week
- **Compliance Impact:** How it affects regulatory requirements
- **Cost:** Monthly software costs
- **Provider Satisfaction:** How staff feel about these tools

## 4. Rank the Solutions

Create a ranked list sorted by:
- **Speed to implement** (fastest first)
- **Ongoing leverage** (highest time savings)
- **Ease of adoption** (least resistance from staff)

Focus on:
- Which training/documentation investments pay off fastest
- Which have the highest ongoing leverage
- Which are easiest to implement with minimal disruption

## Research Focus

Look for:
- Industry benchmarks on training time
- Case studies of successful training implementations
- LMS platforms used in healthcare/aesthetics
- AI documentation tools in similar industries
- Staff satisfaction surveys
- Compliance requirements specific to med spas

## Constraints

Focus on solutions that can be:
- Deployed in under 30 days
- Less than $1,000/month in ongoing costs
- Minimal disruption to daily operations
- Staff can adopt with <2 hours of training

## Output Format

1. **Current state assessment** with specific time/cost burdens
2. **Ranked table** of all solutions by ROI/effort ratio
3. **Deep-dive** on top 5 solutions with implementation steps
4. **Vendor recommendations** with pricing and features
5. **Implementation roadmap** (what to do in month 1, 2, 3)

## Priority

Prioritize solutions that:
- Reduce owner involvement the most
- Have measurable compliance or quality benefits
- Scale as the practice grows
- Staff actually want to use
