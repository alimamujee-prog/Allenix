# Prompt 4: Medical Spa Marketing, Patient Acquisition & Retention Challenges

Research how medical spas and aesthetic practices currently acquire and retain patients, and where their marketing efforts fall short. I need to understand:

## 1. Patient Acquisition Channels
- What channels drive the most new patients (Google, social, referrals, etc.)
- Cost per new patient by channel
- What's working now vs 2-3 years ago
- Where they're wasting marketing spend

## 2. Conversion Bottlenecks
- Website inquiry to booking conversion rates
- Consultation to treatment conversion rates
- Where prospects fall off in the funnel
- What objections prevent booking

## 3. Retention & Lifetime Value Challenges
- Typical patient retention rates and churn
- How often patients should return vs actually return
- What causes patients to stop coming back
- Re-booking rates and why patients don't schedule next treatment

## 4. Review & Reputation Management
- How important reviews are to their business
- How many practices actively generate reviews
- How they handle negative reviews
- What impact reviews have on new patient acquisition

## 5. Content & Social Media Burden
- What content marketing they're trying to do
- How much time it takes
- Whether it drives actual business
- What they wish they could do but can't

## 6. Referral Program Challenges
- How many have structured referral programs
- Why referral programs succeed or fail
- How they track and reward referrals

## Research Focus
Focus on what's NOT working and what they wish they could fix. Include specific metrics and benchmarks. Look for:
- Conversion rate benchmarks
- Lifetime value studies
- Churn and retention data
- Marketing attribution studies
- Review generation case studies

## Sources to Check
- Medical spa marketing benchmarks
- Patient acquisition cost studies
- Conversion optimization case studies
- Review platform data (Google, Yelp, RealSelf)
- Industry marketing surveys

## Output Format
Return findings with:
- Specific conversion rates at each funnel stage
- Dollar impact of each challenge
- What they've tried that didn't work
- What they wish existed but doesn't
- Case studies of practices that solved these challenges

## Priority Areas
Prioritize findings that show clear revenue impact or missed revenue opportunities.
