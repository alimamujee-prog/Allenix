## Current Offer Summary

Allenix installs a **Patient Revenue Recovery System** for med spa and aesthetic practice groups.

External ICP:

**$5M-$25M multi-location med spa and aesthetic practice groups.**

Internal flexibility:

Accept $3M+ single-location clinics when the pain is clearly retention, no-shows, missed inbound, rebooking, or dormant patient reactivation.

Public claim:

**Most $5M-$25M med spa groups we model are leaking at least $150K-$600K/year in avoidable missed visits and one-and-done patients.**

V1 levers:

1. Show-up rate.
2. Rebooking rate.
3. Missed / mishandled inbound.
4. Dormant patient reactivation.
