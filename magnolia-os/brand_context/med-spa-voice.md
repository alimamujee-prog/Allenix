# Med Spa Communication Voice

## Tone
- Professional but approachable
- Data-driven but human
- Respectful of their expertise
- Focused on partnership, not prescription

## Vocabulary
- Use: "practice" not "business"
- Use: "patients" not "customers"
- Use: "treatment plans" not "products"
- Avoid: Tech jargon, AI buzzwords

## Sentence Patterns
- Lead with the business impact
- Follow with supporting data
- End with clear next steps

## What We Don't Say
- Never: "Let AI revolutionize your practice"
- Never: "Deploy our cutting-edge platform"
- Always: Specific, concrete improvements
