# Med Spa Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

## The Target practice
- **Revenue**: 3M - 25M annual revenue
- **Locations**: Multi-location (2-20 offices) groups
- **Core Pain**: Retention hemorrhaging, high no-show rates, inconsistent rebooking
- **Decision Maker**: Founder-CEO or Managing Doctor

## Psychographics
- Values patient outcomes and practice reputation above all
- Skeptical of "marketing agencies" promising leads
- Frustrated by front-desk technology adoption
- Seeks "Self-Running" practice efficiency
