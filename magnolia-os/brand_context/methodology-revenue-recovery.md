# Allenix Methodology: Patient Revenue Recovery

## The Three-Phase Sprint
1. **Map**: Quantify every dollar leaking from the patient journey (no-shows, missed rebooking, dormancy).
2. **Build**: Install the agentic workflows that automate the outreach and response loops.
3. **Run**: Monitor, optimize, and report recovered revenue month-over-month.

## Benchmarks
- **Average Treatment Value**: 500
- **No-Show Cost**: 150 - 500 per slot
- **Recovery Target**: 15-20% improvement in 90 days
