| The Automation and Data Enrichment Engine
Clay handles the backend processes that human reps (or RevOps teams) typically
manage manually:
● Data Enrichment: Clay enriches Jack’s captured contact information in real time.
When Jack gets a name and email, Clay automatically pulls in ﬁrmographic details,
LinkedIn proﬁles, and other key data points.
● CRM Automation: Clay ensures that qualiﬁed contacts and opportunities are
created, enriched, and routed into the CRM (in this case, HubSpot) with no manual
intervention.
● Deduplication and Validation: It also checks whether a contact or company already
exists in the CRM, ensuring no duplicates and maintaining a clean database.
